Regularization is used to prevent models from becoming too complex.

In Machine Learning, complex models often memorize training data instead of learning patterns.
This leads to the problem of
Overfitting.

Regularization solves this by penalizing large weights.

L1 Regularization (Lasso)

Penalty:

λ (|w₁| + |w₂| + |w₃|)

Effect:
1.  pushes some weights exactly to zero
2.  automatically performs feature selection
This is called Lasso Regression.
